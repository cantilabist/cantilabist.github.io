Preparation

Inject a bacterial suspension (PBS) into the microfluidic chamber and allow suﬀicient time (10–15 minutes) for binding to the cantilever surface.

  • Flush chamber with PBS
  • Inject live bacteria and immobilize with chemical linker
  • Wait for 15 minutes for binding

Bacteria in PBS

  • Rinse with PBS to remove unattached bacteria and record cantilever fluctuations

Bacteria in LB

  • Replace PBS with growth medium LB to promote metabolism
  • Monitor fluctuations w/ laser system

Ampicillin

  • Inject the antibiotic
  • Wait 15-20 minutes for the bacteria to die
  • Monitor new fluctuations

LB wash

  • Flush with LB to confirm baterial death
